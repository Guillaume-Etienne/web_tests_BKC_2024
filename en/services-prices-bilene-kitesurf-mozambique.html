Kitesurfing Trips in Mozambique

We offer complete trips to the best spots on the southern coast of Mozambique. You can discover them on our dedicated page. Prices vary according to many parameters, we suggest you make an estimate. To give a basic idea, indicative only, for a group of 4 people, the average is around 100€/day/person, including accommodation, transport, guiding on the spots, beach boy, and 2 meals per day.
